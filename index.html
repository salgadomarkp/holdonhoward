About Us

The Band

Hold on Howard is a Lehigh-Valley based pop punk band officially formed in 2019. It consists of lead vocalist Zach Hartrum, lead guitarist Matthew Hager, rhythm guitarist Kyle Clark, bassist Mark Salgado, and drummer Dimitri Pandelios. We take influence from bands such as Green Day, Nirvana, Mayday Parade, Jimmy Eat World, The Ramones, and Good Charlotte.

Zach Hartrum

Zach was born in New Jersey. He was heavily influenced by his father's love for music. He joined his first band in junior high school. Zach says that was the first time he was actually passionate about something besides video games.

Kyle Clark

Kyle has his roots in metal, rock, and punk. He has been influenced by his father's interest in bands such as Metallica, Nirvana, and The Offspring. Kyle began by producing his own electronic music before deciding to learn the guitar.

Mark Salgado

Mark was born in the Lehigh Valley. He grew up surrounded by music and fell asleep to The Who as a baby. He began playing bass at the age of sixteen and joined his first band shortly thereafter. Mark cites Rush's Geddy Lee as one of his biggest influences. Aside from music, he takes interest in sports, aviation, and writing.

Dimitri Pandelios

Dimitri was also born in the Lehigh Valley. Like the other band members, he grew up in a musical family with his father and sister. He started out on bass and guitar, but began regular drum lessons while he was in middle school. Dimitri's favorite drummer is Travis Barker from blink-182. Aside from music, Dimitri enjoys watching movies.
